## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What devices does AFHAM support?**
A: iPhone and iPad with iOS 17 or later. Universal app optimized for all screen sizes.

**Q: Can I use AFHAM without internet?**
A: Basic document viewing works offline. Analysis requires internet connection. Pro/Enterprise plans include offline analysis.

**Q: How accurate is the AI analysis?**
A: Accuracy varies by document type and complexity. Generally 85-95% accurate for structured documents, 70-85% for complex research papers.

**Q: What languages does AFHAM support?**
A: Full support for Arabic and English. Additional languages planned for future releases.

### Privacy Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Documents processed temporarily in Google's secure cloud. Local copies encrypted on your device. No permanent cloud storage.

**Q: Can BrainSAIT access my documents?**
A: No. Documents are encrypted and only you have the decryption keys. BrainSAIT staff cannot access your content.

**Q: How long is my data kept?**
A: You control retention period (30-90 days). Data automatically deleted per your settings. You can delete immediately anytime.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why is processing slow sometimes?**
A: Processing speed depends on document size, complexity, and internet speed. Large documents (>20MB) may take 3-5 minutes.

**Q: Can I process multiple documents simultaneously?**
A: Yes, up to 3 documents can be processed concurrently. Pro users get up to 10 concurrent processing slots.

**Q: What if the AI gives wrong information?**
A: Always verify critical information from source documents. AI provides analysis suggestions, not definitive facts.
